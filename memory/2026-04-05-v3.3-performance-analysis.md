## 🎯 根本问题

### 问题1: 提示词数量过多导致混淆

**prompts.yaml**的设计问题:
```yaml
# v3.2 (简洁，效果好)
prompts:
  - dark black polluted water with low luminance on river surface
  - blackish gray sewage water in urban river channel
  - opaque black turbid contaminated water area

# v3.3 (详细，但性能下降)
prompts:
  - perfectly clear transparent river water with no color tint
  - clean unpolluted water showing natural blue gray tone
  - crystal clear water surface without any abnormal hue
  - ... (共8个)
```

**分析**:
- ✅ 简洁描述更容易被SigLIP2准确匹配
- ❌ 过多描述可能导致特征分散，反而降低准确率

### 问题2: negative prompts未被使用

`prompts.yaml`中有详细的`negative`字段，但代码**只使用`prompts`字段**！

查看代码：
```python
prompts = cfg.get("prompts", [cls_name.replace("_", " ")])
texts.extend(prompts)  # 只使用prompts，忽略negative
```

**negative prompts完全被浪费了！**
